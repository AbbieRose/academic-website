Hi, I'm Abbie. I'm an interdsciplinary researcher, a reformed software engineer and mathematician, a casual athlete and serious sports lover. I'm unendingly curious how people with no programming experience understand, use and learn about computer science, statistics and more generally, smart technologies.

I'm a PhD Student at the University of Colorado Boulder where I am advised by Ben Shapiro and Shaun Kane. Before that, I was a teacher for Girls Who Code, a software engineer at Goldman Sachs, and a student in Mathematics and Computer Science at Williams College.

In my free time, you can find me in the Colorado mountains trying to ignore my favorite sports teams.
